# Playable Characters

Characters are the most valuable assets in the game. They can be acquired through drops, trades, or direct purchase from the shop. A player can equip **only one character at a time**, and that character's stats are added directly to the player's own base attributes during battles and other calculations.

To equip a character, use `/equip <character_name>`. If another character is already equipped, they will be unequipped first.
